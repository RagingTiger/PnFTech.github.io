DVM School Selection

TEssay Analytics


We are excited to announce a new TEssay Analytics service: DVM School Selection (DSS)!

As the name implies, this service is specifically designed for students who are applying for DVM programs in North America and Europe. With our custom DVM Application Score (DAS), this service offers a rigorous and strategic way to help you understand which programs are objectively the best choices for your application. By integrating up to 10 factors, the DAS is tailored to your profile and school interests. It directly and objectively shows not only the comprehensive picture of all the available programs, but also the relative advantage and/or disadvantage of each program.

How does the DSS service work?

DSS service is provided to you in the form of a report and personal consulting:

  • Upon purchasing the DSS service, you will be asked to provide relevant personal information and school interests.
  • After receiving your information, we will produce a report that contains schools we recommend and suggestions on how to prepare your application. Additionally, the report shows you how the schools are selected quantitatively by presenting the raw data (DAS, admissions, and programs), data visualization, and analysis of all the available schools.
  • In the end, through the personal consulting, we will answer your questions and help you make the final decision on which schools to apply to.
  • The whole service lasts 3 to 4 weeks.
