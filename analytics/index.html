TEssay Analytics

Strategic School Selection

We are excited to announce a new family of TEssay services: TEssay Analytics!

These new analytics services leverage the power of Data Science in Python and college admissions data to help customers develop the objectively best college application strategy: what schools to apply to, and what application factors to improve.

For many people, college is one of the most important investments they will make in their life. This one decision, this investment can forever alter the course of your life. During the last 3 years at TEssay working diligently and passionately with our customers as they navigate the college admissions process, we found that the college admissions consulting industry is strongly lacking something crucial: a rigorous application strategy. From our previous experience with and love of mathematics, data science, programming, and general computer science, we decided to create a family of services that will provide students with the evidence they need to make the objectively best decisions regarding their college admissions process.

After several months of hard work, the first service of TEssay Analytics is here: Strategic School Selection (aka Triple S).

As the name implies, with the Triple S service, based on our own custom Application ROI index, we can help you understand which schools are actually better investments (and in some cases which are relatively worse) for your college application. This one index used together with ranking, tuition, or admission rate data, directly shows not only the big picture of all the schools systematically, but also the relative investment advantage and/or disadvantage of each school.

How does the Triple S service work?

Triple S service is provided to you in the form of a report. Upon purchasing the Triple S service, you will receive a report that contains the Application ROI and college admissions data, data visualization and analysis of the school tier you select. We divide the top 50 universities into 5 tiers based on ranking, and each report contains the data analysis of one tier:

  • Tier 1 Report: Admission data analysis on schools with rankings from 1-10
  • Tier 2 Report: Admission data analysis on schools with rankings from 11-20
  • Tier 3 Report: Admission data analysis on schools with rankings from 21-30
  • Tier 4 Report: Admission data analysis on schools with rankings from 31-40
  • Tier 5 Report: Admission data analysis on schools with rankings from 41-50

Price: 718 Yuan (or 110 USD) per report

Note:

  1. The rankings are based on 2020 Best National University Rankings by US News.
  2. Since there are schools tied in rankings, every tier may contain more or less than 10 schools.
  3. College admissions data are from the fall 2020 entering class. They are the latest and most complete admission data.
  4. Since the Triple S service is still in beta version, the price is 50% off for the first two months after the initial release. Starting in October 2021, the price will return to the original: 718 Yuan per report.
