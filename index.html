Essay Writing. By using the essay scoring system (Score My Essay) combined with our professional writing experience, we can provide more scientific and professional essay writing service to undergraduate and graduate applicants. Score My Essay was developed by our team of researchers using natural language processing to calculate word relevance and density of the personal essays of students admitted to top schools. Compared to traditional way of writing, this system allows us to help undergraduate and graduate applicants improve the objective quality of their admissions essays.

If you choose to DIY your own essay, you can still use Score My Essay to compare it with the essays that worked for top schools. It's free now!
