How does the DSS service work?

DSS service is provided to you in the form of a report and personal consulting:

  • Upon purchasing the DSS service, you will be inquired to provide relevant personal information and school interests.
  • After receiving your information, we will produce a report that contains schools we recommend for your choice and suggestions on how to prepare your application. Additionally, the report shows you how the schools are selected quantitatively by presenting the raw data (DAS, admissions, and programs), data visualization, and analysis of all the available schools.
  • In the end, through the personal consulting, we will answer your questions and help you make the final decision on which schools to apply.
  • The whole service lasts 3 to 4 weeks.
