TEssay Analytics Corporation

We use data science to optimize your college or graduate school application.

What we do at TEssay Analytics Corporation (TAC), after much distillation and extraction, is ultimately simple: We provide evidence-based answers to questions relevant to college and graduate school applications. And we do this all using the most powerful analytic techniques in the history of mankind: data science and machine learning.

Equipped with four years of professional experience, our own graduate experience in America, plus our knowledge and skills in data science and machine learning, we are here to help you optimize your application.

  • Essay Writing

    • By using the essay scoring system (Score My Essay) combined with our professional writing experience, we can provide more scientific and professional essay writing service to undergraduate and graduate applicants. Score My Essay was developed by our team of researchers using natural language processing to calculate word relevance and density of the personal essays of students admitted to top schools. Compared to traditional ways of writing, this system allows us to help undergraduate and graduate applicants improve the objective quality of their admissions essays.

    If you choose to DIY your own essay, you can still use Score My Essay to compare it with the essays that worked for top schools. It's free now!

  • Strategic School Selection

    • Undergraduate School Selection

      • This service helps undergraduate applicants select schools by using quantitative analysis. Based on our own custom Application ROI index, we help you understand which schools among the TOP 50 are actually better investments for your application. This one index used together with ranking, tuition, or admission rate data, directly shows not only the big picture of all the TOP 50 schools systematically, but also the relative application advantage and/or disadvantage of each school.

    • DVM School Selection

      • This service is specifically designed for students that are facing the extremely selective DVM admissions process. Our own custom DVM Application Score, tailored to your personal profile and interests, will help you select objectively the best schools to apply. Meanwhile, suggestions on how to prepare your application are provided to leverage your advantages and improve your admission probability.
